02 · Forecast modes

01

Wind forecast

The full week ahead, mapped to colour. Calm days glow a cool blue-green. As wind builds through yellow and amber, stormy days burn deep red. A single glance from the couch tells you when the window opens.

02

Weather forecast

Overcast grey bleeding into golden yellow for sun, a deep blue for heavy rain and purple for storms. Seven days of sky conditions rendered as a continuous gradient across the instrument.

03

Swell forecast

Swell height and period translated into light. Flat days sit in pale green; as groundswell builds the gradient shifts through teal and deep cyan. For surfers and kiteboarders who need more than just wind.

03 · Live data

01

Live wind speed

Switch to live mode and ISOBAR shows current wind speed at your spot in real time. Hang it vertically and the gradient fills from the bottom up as wind builds. A simple, immediate reading of right now.

02

Live tide

The tidal cycle rendered as a shifting gradient. High tide glows full and bright; as the tide drops the light recedes. Essential context for any session that depends on water depth or tidal flow.

04 · Details

Day markers

Engraved tick marks divide the gradient into days, with noon and midnight clearly marked. A single flashing LED indicates the current hour, so you can pinpoint exactly where you are in the forecast at a glance.

Mode switch

The engraved ISOBAR logo badge on the top surface is a capacitive touch button. One press cycles through forecast modes and live data views. An ambient light sensor on the same face automatically adjusts brightness to the room, bright in daylight and dim at night.

Installation

Slotted keyhole mounts on the back panel sit flush against the wall, with channels that guide the power cable out of sight. Hang it horizontally for a 7-day forecast, or rotate it vertical to show live wind or tide. The display adapts automatically.

Solid timber

Each enclosure is hand-finished from ecologically sourced solid timber. The grain, tone and character vary from lamp to lamp. No two are identical.
